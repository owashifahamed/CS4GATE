Which of the following is NOT one of Coffman's necessary conditions for deadlock?

Operating System Deadlocks Deadlock Conditions
MCQ2026Practice

Choose an Option

Answer

Preemption

Theory

Deadlock

Coffman's conditions are Mutual Exclusion, Hold and Wait, No Preemption and Circular Wait.

Solution

  • Mutual Exclusion is required.
  • Hold and Wait is required.
  • No Preemption is required.
  • Circular Wait is required.
  • Therefore Preemption is not a deadlock condition.
