The regular expression (0+1)* represents:

Theory of Computation Regular Expressions Language Representation
MCQ2026Practice

Choose an Option

Answer

All binary strings

Theory

Regular Expressions

The Kleene star allows zero or more occurrences of symbols from the given set.

Solution

  • (0+1) means either 0 or 1.
  • * means zero or more repetitions.
  • Any combination of 0s and 1s is possible.
  • Hence it represents all binary strings.
