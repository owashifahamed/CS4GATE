Which machine accepts Context-Free Languages?

Theory of Computation Context Free Grammar CFG
MCQ2026Practice

Choose an Option

Answer

Pushdown Automaton

Theory

Pushdown Automata

A Pushdown Automaton (PDA) is a finite automaton equipped with a stack.

Solution

  • Regular languages are accepted by DFA/NFA.
  • Context-Free Languages require additional memory.
  • PDA provides a stack for memory.
  • Therefore PDA accepts CFLs.
