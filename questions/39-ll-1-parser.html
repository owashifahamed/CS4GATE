Left recursion must be eliminated before constructing an LL(1) parser because:

Compiler Design Parsing LL(1) Parser
MCQ2026Practice

Choose an Option

Answer

It causes infinite recursion

Theory

LL(1) Parsing

Recursive-descent parsers cannot handle left-recursive grammars directly.

Solution

  • Consider A ->AA alpha | beta
  • The parser repeatedly expands A.
  • This leads to infinite recursion.
  • Therefore left recursion must be removed.
