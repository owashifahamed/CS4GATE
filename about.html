About us

Built for focused GATE CS preparation

CS4GATE is a focused practice platform for GATE Computer Science preparation. It keeps public questions, signed-in practice, bookmarks, discussions, quiz tools, mock tests, and progress tracking in one clean workspace.

Mission

Make revision calmer and more organized by turning scattered questions into a searchable practice system.

Practice Tools

Subject, chapter, year, and topic filters help you move from broad preparation to precise weak-area practice.

Account Data

When you are signed in, the platform can save attempts, bookmarks, revision marks, notes, comments, quiz history, and mock activity.
