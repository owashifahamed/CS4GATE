Privacy policy

How CS4GATE handles your study data

This policy explains how CS4GATE handles sign-in, progress, bookmarks, notes, comments, contact messages, quiz history, and mock test activity.

Account Data

When you create an account, CS4GATE stores your name, email, hashed password, role, plan, and target year in the database.

Study Activity

Signed-in activity such as progress, bookmarks, revision flags, notes, likes, comments, quiz results, and mock test history may be saved so your dashboard can show your learning status.

Contact Messages

Messages submitted through the contact form are stored for admin review. Comments may be moderated before they appear publicly.

Email

CS4GATE may send account verification, password reset, contact, and moderation notification emails using domain email addresses such as no-reply@cs4gate.com and support@cs4gate.com.

Browser Data

CS4GATE may also use browser storage for theme preference, recent searches, and local interface state. External CDN assets such as icons may be requested by your browser.

Data Updates

Contact the site admin if you need account data corrected or removed. You can also clear browser-only data from your browser settings.
